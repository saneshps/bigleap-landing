Frequently Asked Questions

What animation services do you offer?

At Bigleap, we provide end-to-end animation services, from storyboarding and pre-production to 2D and 3D animation, product explainers, and post-production — our animation services cover the entire project.

How do animated videos help in business branding?

Animated videos boost brand recall, simplify complex messaging, and create emotional connections — helping your business stand out across social media, websites, and presentations with a memorable visual identity.

How long does it take to create an animation video?

Production time depends on scope and style. A short explainer typically takes 2–4 weeks, while full 3D campaigns can run 6–12 weeks from concept and storyboard through to final delivery.

How much does it cost to produce a video?

Cost varies by animation style, duration, and complexity. We offer flexible packages and a free demo video so you can see our quality before committing — reach out for a tailored quote.
